About me

I came to coding and Web development almost by accident: In my final semester of undergrad a beginning HTML was the only class that fit my schedule. I took to it immediately. It was like playing with Legos again: write the code and see the result!

After graduation I continued to tinker with HTML and CSS, but I decided to take it seriously after getting laid off from a job I wasn't passionate about in the summer of 2012.

My time in the IDM program has been transformative for me. I'm much more advanced in design and coding, and I have a more rounded skillset that includes UX design, project management and marketing.

And the transformation wasn't just in education. Here's a list of things that happened in my life since I started the IDM program:

  • Got a job
  • Moved
  • Got a better job
  • Got married
  • Became a father
  • Moved again
  • Got a promoted

Going forward I want to pursue becoming a front-end developer. I'm passionate about writing efficient, semantic code and expanding my knowledge to new tools and languages.
